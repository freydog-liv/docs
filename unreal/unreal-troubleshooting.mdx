## Recording button stays at 00:00 and video isn't saved

- Verify your TrackingId has been set in the DefaultEngine.ini

  ![Image](/images/image.png)
- Your TrackingID can be found in your game info on the [LIV dashboard](https://dashboard.liv.tv/)

![Trackingid Dashboard](/images/trackingid_dashboard.png)
