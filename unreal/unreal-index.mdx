## What is LIV?

LIV was founded in 2016. We're on a mission to empower content creators, and game developers to share their experiences in VR. We're the industry leading solution today, and the [official solution for Meta Quest ](https://developers.meta.com/horizon/blog/meta-quest-developer-LIV-mixed-reality-capture-support-sdk/).

We want to enable you to empower your players & creators to make great content and share it to their favorite platforms. We do this through providing developer tooling that unlocks our camera features inside your application.
